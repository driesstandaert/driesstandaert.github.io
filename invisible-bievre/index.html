Invisible Bièvre

The Bièvre (Old French for “beaver”) is a historic river that once flowed openly through Paris as the city’s second waterway. Today however, it runs hidden underneath the streets.

Together with a group of friends, technologists and artists we developed an idea to revive the Bièvre's heritage and historical significance through an interactive AR/VR app. It was an opportunity for me to learn about immersive experiences whilst working towards something meaningful.

We created a proof of concept where I focused on the AR/VR development side and some 3D design. It involved a lot of trial and error, but seeing it come together in the end was incredibly satisfying.

Visit invisible-bievre.netlify.app to see the Proof of Concept
or invisible-bievre.com for more information.
