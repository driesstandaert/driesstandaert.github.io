Echelon Starter UI Kit

I created Echelon as a compact Figma UI kit that works as a solid starting point for digital projects. A foundational design system that is easy to customise, is consistent and scalable, aligned with coding conventions, but without becoming bloated or difficult for others to understand and use.

It includes the essential components and styles that are commonly needed in modern interfaces. It's using semantic tokens for colours and spacing and every component (and/or variant) comes with its well-defined states and properties.
